Abstract

This talk is dedicated to showing people how to use LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter, and personal websites to achieve self-promotion in order to prepare themselves for today’s data science job market. I will take the data hackathon as an example and provide hands-on instructions on how to create posts for acknowledging your work at the data hackathon.
